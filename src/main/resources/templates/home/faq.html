Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for the main user actions in the portal.

How do I join an event?

Register from the event details page after signing in as a participant.

How are events approved?

Club heads submit events, then admins review and approve or reject them.

When do I get a pass?

Free events can generate passes immediately after registration. Paid events can generate them after payment completion logic is added.
