About Eventra

A focused portal for RPSS clubs, approvals, registrations, media, passes, and feedback.

For Students

Discover upcoming events, register quickly, download passes, and submit feedback after attending.

For Club Heads

Create event proposals, track status, review registrations, and keep your club activity pipeline visible.

For Admins

Approve events, manage clubs and users, and generate event-level reports from one admin hub.
